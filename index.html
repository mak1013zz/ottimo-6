Our story
Luxury Taste. Warm Hospitality.

Nestled in the heart of Hayatabad, Cafe Ottimo was born from a passion to bring the world's authentic flavours to the people of Peshawar. Every dish we serve carries a promise — of quality, of taste, and of an experience you won't forget.

"In an effort to bring authentic cuisines and tantalising tastes for the people of Peshawar, we humbly present to you, Cafe Ottimo! We hope to make your dining experience joyful and memorable."
